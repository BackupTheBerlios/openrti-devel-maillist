Does _federateHandleSet only contain the own federate handle like sketched 
above?

Discovering federates is not possible.
If you know the federates names you can query their handles, but you can only 
query them if they are already joined and your federate technically knows 
about them.
If I had to do this, I would synchonize to all federates at the beginning, 
then collect the federate handles and synchronize later on the known ones.
